PLNT, The nap assistant

For this project we got the assignment to design a product that needs to improve the sleep quality of the user.

I worked in a group and we developed PLNT, The Nap Assistent.

It's meant for students who take powernaps to make sure they will wake up after they've passed all the different sleep cycles.

Whitin this project I was responsible for the product photography, the product video, it's post-production and I've made the tracker.

I've learned how to design and build a physical product.

I've also learned how to handle challenges whitin a group dynamic.
